Introduction：

Durham House of Pet（DHP）） was established in 2003. For 16 years we have been committed to providing high quality service and high quality products. Our annual sales volume continues to increase by 4-6%. The head office is located in California.

Why Use Us：

The prices of our pet stores are suitable and the quality is right. We have three permanent staff, each of whom can provide you with high quality services, including product selection, pet grooming, and pet consultants. We receive more than 20,000 customers each year.
